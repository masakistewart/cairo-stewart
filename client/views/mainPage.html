About Me

I have been described as a very “chill person”. I have done my best work in environments where every team member is just as excited to accomplish and complete the task at hand. I am a big believer in appreciating the little things so I would like to work where my team and I can mutually benefit from one another - this would consist a jovial, casual atmosphere that primarily gets the work done. Push the code, run the tests, complete the tasks, drink some more coffee and repeat.

The software technologies I am passionate in have coincided with the work that I have done in the past. More recently I have been focusing on learning the newest/sturdiest frameworks. Being Versatile is something that as a Full Stack Developer is very important. I tend to gravitate towards the backend, /Node/Express/Postgres/Heroku/GoogleAnalytics/ all the way :)
